=====> Chapter 4: Resiliency
Connection Pooling

Problem: Which of the following are benefits of connection pooling?

Solution: 1: New operations can be serviced with pre-existing connections, so a new connection doesn't have to be created each time.
          2: A large influx of operations can be handled more quickly with a pool of existing connections.


=====> Chapter 4: Resiliency

Solution: When our application is using a Write Concern more durable than w: 1.
